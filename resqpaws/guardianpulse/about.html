About Our Mission

resQpaws bridges the gap between animal welfare organizations and citizens reporting emergencies.

Our Vision

resQpaws is built to act as the visual nervous system of animal rescue coordinates. Often, stray, injured, or stray animals are left on roadsides because citizens don't know who to call, or how to describe the emergency severity.

Our platform utilizes simulated AI-assisted visual severity scanner details to assign emergency levels (Critical, High, Medium, Low) and triggers immediate alerts to matching nearby NGOs based on active geo-locations.

24/7 Support

Constant support vectors linking citizens to rescuers.

Zero Cost

Free platform powered by open source mapping grids.

Our Core Philosophy

The values guiding our networks every single day.

Integrity & Speed

Every minute matters when an animal is bleeding. We route coordinates instantly to ensure minimal rescue wait times.

Compassionate Welfare

Treating every stray animal with the same diagnostic detail and dignity as a home pet. Every life matters.

Community Trust

Providing transparent tracking timelines so citizens can witness the exact rescue status of the animals they reported.

resQpaws

Empowering citizens to report animal emergencies and coordinating shelters dynamically using AI-Assisted assessments.
