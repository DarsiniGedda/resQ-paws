Diagnostic Rules

  • 🔴 Critical: Severe open fractures, heavy blood discharge, or unconscious state. Immediate dispatch triggered.
  • 🟠 High: Noticeable bleeding cuts or severe limping issues. NGO requested within 1 hour.
  • 🟡 Medium: Rashes, sickness, malnutrition or dehydration signs. NGO requested within 24 hours.
  • 🟢 Low: Found healthy but stranded in observation regions.
