How It Works

Coordinate animal rescue processes instantly with simple steps.

📢

1. Report Incident

Upload an image of the injured or stranded animal. Select coordinates on the map.

🤖

2. Simulated AI Scan

Our simulated AI-Assisted Assessment automatically evaluates injury severity and distress levels.

🚑

3. NGO Dispatched

Nearby NGOs receive details immediately and send rescue vehicles with status tracking.
